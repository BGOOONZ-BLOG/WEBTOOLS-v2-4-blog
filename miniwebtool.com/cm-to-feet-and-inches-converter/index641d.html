About Cm to Feet and Inches Converter

The Cm to Feet and Inches Conversion Calculator is used to convert centimeters to feet and inches.

FAQ

To convert from cm to feet and inches, use the following two conversion equations:

1 cm = 0.3937 inches

and

1 foot = 12 inches

The equation is:

180 cm = 180 x 0.3937 inches = 70.86614 inches

70.86614 div 12 = 5 and 70.86614 mod 12 = 10.86614

so 180 cm = 5 feet 10.86614 inches

You can enter 180 cm above to make this tool calculate for you.
